🌙 Privacy Policy — Little Noor

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Little Noor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our app and social media content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Our app and social media content is designed for educational purposes for children aged 4 and above, under parental supervision.

We may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (app performance, feature usage)
  • Device information (operating system, app version)
  • Language preferences

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide educational Islamic content appropriate for children
  • Send notifications about new content (with parental consent)

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Little Noor complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Parents may contact us to review, delete, or stop collecting their child's information.

4. Social Media Content

Our social media channels (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Telegram, WhatsApp) publish Islamic educational content for children. We do not collect data through social media interactions beyond what each platform provides to content creators.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information. We do not sell, trade, or transfer user information to third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services including Apple App Store and Google Play Store, which have their own privacy policies.
